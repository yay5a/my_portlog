A smart recipe discovery platform that helps users find, save, and organize culinary inspirations based on preferences and dietary needs.

## Features

-   Personalized recipe recommendations
-   Dietary restriction filtering (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
-   Meal planning calendar
-   Shopping list generation
-   Recipe scaling for different serving sizes

## Technology

Developed with Next.js, Firebase, and integrates with Spoonacular API for recipe data.

[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/yay5a/fineda-nomnom)

_Note: Currently in beta testing with limited features. Full launch expected Q4 2025._
